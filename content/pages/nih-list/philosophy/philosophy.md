# Philosophy
At the end, it was always about carefully creating new problems to avoid solving
the old ones.


## Jainism
Embarrasingly long because I was born into this.

- **Extremism in Jainism** People think it is just about not stepping on bugs,
but it is actually about the terrifying logistics of Karma.
- **Anekantavad** A very sophisticated way of saying "I am not wrong".


## (other) Indian Schools of Thought
A series of increasingly complex architectural debates on 'Self'.

- **Ajivika** Accepting that the universe is a pre-compiled program.
- **Yoga** Overclocking the brain by manually killing background thoughts.
